## 2. Logistics Cost Analysis

### Transportation Network Requirements

**Inbound:**
- [ ] Where do products originate? (list top origins)
- [ ] Primary inbound modes:
  - [ ] Truckload
  - [ ] LTL
  - [ ] Rail/intermodal
  - [ ] Air cargo
  - [ ] Ocean container
- [ ] Inbound volume (containers, trucks per week): _______

**Outbound:**
- [ ] Customer distribution map (where do orders ship?)
- [ ] Primary outbound modes:
  - [ ] Parcel (UPS, FedEx, USPS)
  - [ ] LTL
  - [ ] Truckload
  - [ ] Rail/intermodal
- [ ] Outbound volume: _______
- [ ] Service level requirements (delivery times): _______

### Logistics Cost Study Components

For each location under consideration, analyze:

- [ ] **Inbound transportation costs**
  - Freight rates from major origin points
  - Modal options and costs
  - Transit times

- [ ] **Outbound transportation costs**
  - Parcel zone analysis (% of orders by zone)
  - LTL rates to key markets
  - Truckload rates to key destinations

- [ ] **Market coverage analysis**
  - Population reached within 1-day ground
  - Population reached within 2-day ground
  - % of order volume by delivery zone

- [ ] **Total logistics cost comparison**
  - Annualized inbound costs
  - Annualized outbound costs
  - Cost per order/unit shipped

## 8. Financial Incentives

### Common Distribution Incentives

- [ ] Job creation tax credits
- [ ] Property tax abatements
- [ ] Sales tax exemptions (racking, equipment)
- [ ] Training grants
- [ ] Infrastructure improvements
- [ ] Foreign Trade Zone benefits

### Incentive Reality Check

Distribution projects often receive fewer incentives than manufacturing due to:
- Lower average wages
- Higher turnover
- Less capital intensity

**Focus on:** Locations with strong logistics fundamentals first, then optimize incentives.

### Key Questions

- [ ] Minimum job/wage thresholds for incentives
- [ ] Are distribution jobs eligible, or only "manufacturing"?
- [ ] Timeline for receiving incentives
- [ ] Reporting requirements
- [ ] Clawback provisions

## Next Steps

### Recommended Approach

1. **Define your logistics requirements** using this checklist
2. **Conduct logistics cost modeling** for 3-5 target markets
3. **Shortlist locations** based on logistics cost + labor
4. **Evaluate real estate** options in shortlisted markets
5. **Negotiate incentives** in final candidate locations
