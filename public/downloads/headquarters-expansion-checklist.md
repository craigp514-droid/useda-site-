## 3. Labor Market Evaluation

### For Each Market, Research:

**Talent Supply:**
- [ ] Total labor force size
- [ ] Concentration in your target job categories
- [ ] University graduates in relevant fields
- [ ] Professional certifications/designations
- [ ] Languages spoken (if relevant)

**Talent Competition:**
- [ ] Major employers competing for same talent
- [ ] Industry concentration (good or bad?)
- [ ] Employer of choice reputation in market
- [ ] Poaching risk assessment

**Labor Costs:**
- [ ] Salary benchmarking for key positions
- [ ] Total compensation comparison (benefits, bonus)
- [ ] Wage growth trends
- [ ] Cost of living adjustment implications

**Labor Dynamics:**
- [ ] Unemployment rate for target occupations
- [ ] In-migration vs. out-migration trends
- [ ] Remote work competitive landscape
- [ ] Turnover rates by industry
