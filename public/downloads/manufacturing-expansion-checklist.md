### Key Labor Questions

- [ ] What specific skills and certifications are required?
- [ ] What industries do your ideal candidates come from?
- [ ] What is your shift structure? (1, 2, or 3 shifts)
- [ ] Union or non-union preference?
- [ ] What is your competitive wage position? (market rate, above, below)

### Labor Market Evaluation Criteria

For each location you evaluate, gather:

- [ ] Unemployment rate
- [ ] Labor force participation rate
- [ ] Commute shed population (30, 45, 60 minute drive times)
- [ ] Competing employers and their wage rates
- [ ] Available workforce with required skills
- [ ] Community college and technical training programs
- [ ] Turnover rates in the market
- [ ] Right-to-work status

## 4. Supply Chain Analysis

### Inbound Logistics

- [ ] List top 10 suppliers and their locations
- [ ] Primary transportation modes for raw materials
- [ ] Inventory strategy (JIT, safety stock levels)
- [ ] Import requirements (if applicable):
  - [ ] Port of entry preferences
  - [ ] Foreign Trade Zone benefits
  - [ ] Customs brokerage needs

### Outbound Logistics

- [ ] Customer distribution map (where do products ship?)
- [ ] Primary transportation modes for finished goods
- [ ] Distribution center requirements
- [ ] Service level requirements (delivery times)

### Supply Chain Evaluation Criteria

- [ ] Proximity to key suppliers
- [ ] Proximity to key customers
- [ ] Interstate highway access
- [ ] Rail service availability
- [ ] Air cargo access
- [ ] Port access (if needed)
- [ ] Intermodal facilities
- [ ] Third-party logistics providers

## 7. Financial Incentives

### Types of Incentives to Explore

**State-Level:**
- [ ] Job creation tax credits
- [ ] Investment tax credits
- [ ] Cash grants
- [ ] Training grants
- [ ] Property tax abatements
- [ ] Sales tax exemptions
- [ ] Infrastructure grants

**Local-Level:**
- [ ] Property tax abatements/PILOT
- [ ] Land cost assistance
- [ ] Building grants
- [ ] Permit fee waivers
- [ ] Infrastructure improvements
- [ ] TIF financing

**Utility:**
- [ ] Economic development rates
- [ ] Connection fee waivers
- [ ] Line extension assistance

### Incentive Evaluation Questions

- [ ] What are the minimum thresholds? (jobs, investment)
- [ ] What are the wage requirements?
- [ ] What is the timeline for receiving incentives?
- [ ] What are the clawback provisions?
- [ ] What reporting is required?
- [ ] What is the estimated total incentive value?
- [ ] What is the net present value of incentives?
